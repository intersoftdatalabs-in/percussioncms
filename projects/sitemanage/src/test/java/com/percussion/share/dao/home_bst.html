BST

 

BST was formed in 1989 by a dedicated group of people intent on providing quality consulting services to the financial community.

Prior to this time, the partners were independently involved in all aspects of technology within the financial sector including market data, telecommunications, networking, disaster recovery and all areas of expense management. Within BST, the combined experience of its various members provides customers with a unique blend of talent from which they can derive a multitude of benefits in the areas of system and services analysis, design and selection of new technologies, inventory analysis and cost reduction strategies, contract negotiations and a number of other Information Technology initiatives.

BST has offices in the United States, UK and Europe. Our company provides global products and services in the following five categories:

1. Market Data Consulting
2. Technology Consulting
3. Managed Services
4. ASP Solutions
5. FinOffice5 Software
