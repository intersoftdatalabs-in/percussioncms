Comparing Two Revisions of a Content Item

To compare two revisions of a Content Item:

  1. In the Action Menu of the Content Item, choose View > Compare.

    Rhythmyx opens the Comparison Page. The page lists the most recent available revision of the Content Item in the upper left frame and displays it assembled in a Template format in its Item tab in the right frame. The Content Item is assembled as the default Template displayed in the Select Template drop list in the upper left frame.

  2. To choose a different revision of the Content Item, click [Select Revision] in the upper left frame.

    Rhythmyx opens a list of revisions of the Content Item.

    NOTE: If another user has the Content Item checked out, the most recent revision does not appear in the list because it is not available to you.

  3. To select a revision, click the icon in its row.

    Rhythmyx closes the list of revisions, lists the selected revision in the upper left frame, and displays it assembled in its Item tab in the right frame.

  4. Optionally, in the Select Template drop list, select a different Template for the Content Item.

    Rhythmyx displays the revision assembled as the selected Template in its Item tab in the right frame.

  5. In the lower left frame, click [Select Revision].

    Rhythmyx opens the list of revisions for the Content Item.

  6. Click the icon in a row to select the second revision for comparison.

    Rhythmyx closes the Revision List, lists the selected revision in the lower left frame, and displays it assembled in its Item tab in the right frame.

  7. Optionally, in the revision's Select Template drop list, choose a different Template.

    Rhythmyx displays the revision assembled as the selected Template in its Item tab.

  8. In the right frame, click the Compared tab.

    The tab displays the text fields from both revisions with text changes marked. The upper left revision is treated as the new item; the lower revision is treated as the old item. If you have selected different Templates for the revisions, the Compared tab displays fields that appear on one Template but not the other as new or deleted text.
