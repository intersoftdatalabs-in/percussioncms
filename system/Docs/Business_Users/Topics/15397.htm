How to Move Dependent Items and Reset Active Assembly Links

To move a Content Item to another Site in the following two situations you must use a Force Move action to reset Active Assembly links:

  • If a Content Item is a dependent in an Active Assembly Relationship, and its siteid and folderid are properties of the Relationship.
  • If a Content Item is a dependent in an Active Assembly Relationship, and its siteid is a property of the Relationship, and no other instances of the Content Item appear in other Folders on the Site.

To force a move:

  1. You must be in a Role and Community that has access to the Force Move action. By default, administrators in administrator Communities have access to the Force Move action.
  2. Either
    • In the Content Explorer display pane, right-click on the Content Item and choose Copy.
    • In the Content Explorer navigation pane, right-click on the folder in the new Site and choose Paste > Force Move.

    Or

    • In the Content Explorer display pane, click on the Content Item and drag it to the folder in the new Site. In the menu that opens, choose Force Move.

    The item is moved and the Relationship's links are reset.
